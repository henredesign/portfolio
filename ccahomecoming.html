CCA - HOMECOMING // BRANDING
2022

CCA’s Homecoming and Holiday Fair event was held for the first time on the newly unified San Francisco campus in 2022. The event featured a street party in addition to the annual Holiday Fair featuring handmade goods for sale from alumni, students, faculty, and staff.

I was invited to create the identity around this years Homecoming, with the intention of giving it a whimsical and groovy vibe. Using archival photos of Alumni from the 70’s and vibrant colors, I created these digital collages that were used for signage, banners, and digital marketing materials.
