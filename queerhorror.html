HENRE DESIGN

QUEER MONSTERS // HARDCOVER PUBLICATION
2019

Queer Monsters is a collation of numerous examples of monsters, cryptids villains, scary stories and theory that connects the identities of queer people to the strange and supernatural. Through explorations of queer coding in fictional characters, strange fictions told from a queer perspective, and queering ghost hunting and cryptozoology, this publication will provide a deeper look into the relationship between the queer identity with the strange and weird.
