about

My name is Henry Rivera. I am a Southern California based designer and a recent graduate from the Graphic Design program at California College of The Arts. I’ve created work that ranges from poster design, book design, brand identity, exhibition curation and design, printmaking, and interactive work using HTML and CSS. My work tends to find a bridge between different processes, and incorporates queerness – both in the sense of my LGBT+ identity as well as the idea of the strange or weird with a tendency towards surrealism and paranormal concepts.
