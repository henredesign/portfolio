about

Henry Rivera is a chicano Graphic Designer and artist based in California. He has a BFA in Graphic Design from California College of the Arts and has created work for Galería de la Raza, Yerba Buena Center for the Arts, City of Ventura, California Lutheran University, and more.

With a versatile range of skills in both digital and print design, Henry is experienced in working with printmaking mediums, book binding, and other analog processes. In 2020, he was honored among other top students at CCA on GDUSA’s “Students to Watch” list.
