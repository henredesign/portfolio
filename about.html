HENRE DESIGN

about

My name is Henry Rivera. I am a Southern California based designer currently enrolled in the undergraduate Graphic Design program at California College of The Arts. I’ve created work that ranges from poster design, book design, brand identity, exhibition curation and design, printmaking, and interactive work using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. My work tends to find a bridge between different processes like combining digital mediums like web design with more analog processes like screen printing. I incorporate queerness into my designs both in the sense of LGBT+ identity as well as the idea of the strange or weird, incorporating surrealism and diving into paranormal concepts. I aim to include a piece of myself and my own identity in everything I create.
