CARAVAN FOR THE CHILDREN // CAMPAIGN MATERIALS
2021-2022

Caravan for the Children Campaign, in its first phase, was a national six-month effort to demand the release, reunification and healing of migrant children still being held in ICE custody across the country as a result of the Zero Tolerance policy. Organizers also sought to hold the incoming Biden/Harris administration accountable to the promises outlined in the Biden Immigration Plan.

My role in this campaign was to design campaign materials such as: t-shirts, flags, banners, posters, postcards, and more.

As of 2022, with only 100 children released since the beginning of Bidens presidency, the campaign enters its second phase continuing to demand that these children be Uncaged, Reunified, and Healed.
