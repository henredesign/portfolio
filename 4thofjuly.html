CITY OF VENTURA: 4TH OF JULY // BRANDING
2024

During my employment at gthe City of Ventura, I created the identity for the 47th annual 4th of July Street Fair and Pushem-Pullem Parade. The teams idea was to focus on the new "bubble zone" for the kids that was a big hit the previous year. Aesthetically, the team desired a funky Americana style with a simple color palette and elements that could be shifted around for multiple outputs. This was to differ from previous years with only having one graphic for all outputs.

This design is almost fully illustrated and hand lettered as a call back to the organic hand-lettered posters of the 60's and 70's. The star bubble is the "star" of the design being the prominent element across all project materials!
