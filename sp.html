SCREEN PRINTING // VARIOUS WORKS

A collection of different prints created during my time in CCA. Many of these prints delve into different parts of my own identity focusing on my queerness, body image, and Chicano culture. Others are designed as part of my other interests such as music, conspiracy theories and supernatural stories.

I use screenprinting as an outlet to create work that feels a lot more personal to myself and work that can comfort me. I think it is important for designers to design for themselves as much as they design for others.
