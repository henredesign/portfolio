LUNADA: VITRUAL LITERARY LOUNGE // SOCIAL MEDIA PROMO
2020-2022

Lunada Literary Lounge is a monthly bilingual literary ritual & performance gathering devoted to spoken word, música, song, and story held on the full moon. Now hosted in a virtual space, Lunada is guest curated by dynamic literary artists featuring national and local community poets, artists, performers, and legends of the written word.

As an extension of my work with Galería de la Raza, I create the visual language for each season of Lunada. Since every edition of Lunada brings a different set of voices and ideas, I wanted the visuals of Lunada to be ever-changing, while still holding to a recognizable brand and color palette.
