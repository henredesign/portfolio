BEGINNING TO END // COLLECTION BOOK
2017

Beginning To End is a collection of CD’s found from the East Bay Depot of Creative Reuse in Oakland, California. To showcase this collection, the waveform of the first and last track is being displayed for each album. Then the albums are catergorized by genre of their music, including an “Other” chapter for more obsure music within the collection. To emphasize the diversity of waveform between each track, the design of the book includes a repeated rainbow gradient throughout each page.
