SCANDINAVIAN FESTIVAL // LOGO
2025

The Scandinavian Festival is a celebration of Scandinavian culture held at California Lutheran University in Thousand Oaks, California. I worked with the event coordinators to create an offcial logo for the festival to be used for the upcoming and future events. This design takes a big inspiration from Scandinavian folk art and the style of Rosemaling art, with a color palette inspired by the vibrant Scandinavian country flags.
